## Supplement Accuracy Notes
- Omega-3 evidence level should be "studied-in-canine-cancer" not "studied-in-hsa" (Ogilvie 2000 was lymphoma, not HSA)
- Artemisinin: no canine HSA clinical trial exists, only in vitro + case reports
- Multiple supplements lack cited sources — should add PubMed references
