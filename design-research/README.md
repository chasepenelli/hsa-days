# HSA Days Design Research

Design research for the visual direction of HSA Days — a 30-day guided companion for dog owners navigating hemangiosarcoma.

## Key Principle

> Every site that succeeds with heavy emotional content shares one trait: **generosity with whitespace.** Space signals safety. Crowded = clinical.

---

## Top 10 Reference Sites

### 1. Lap of Love (lapoflove.com)
The closest real-world analog to HSA Days. Veterinary hospice site serving people in the same exact crisis: a pet with a terminal diagnosis. Warm photography, conversational tone, testimonials that validate rather than sell.

**Draw from:** Photography-led heroes, gentle CTAs, section language that feels like a hand on the shoulder.

### 2. DogCancer.com
Shows how medically accurate content and emotional warmth can coexist. "By dog lovers, for dog lovers." Clean reading hierarchy, trust signals without clinical coldness.

**Draw from:** Content-forward layout, long-form reading experience, expert authorship treated warmly not stiffly.

### 3. Grief Refuge App (griefrefuge.com)
The closest structural analog: daily content delivery, private journal, emotional journey tracker. "Safe and sacred space."

**Draw from:** The daily delivery model, mood check-in pattern, "you're not alone" validation framing before any guidance.

### 4. Plum Village (plumvillage.org)
Models how to hold heavy grief content without visual heaviness. Extreme whitespace, natural photography, unhurried typography.

**Draw from:** Breathing room as a design principle, photography of presence and nature, no-urgency scroll experience.

### 5. What's Your Grief (whatsyourgrief.com)
300k+ monthly readers, demonstrates grief content can be highly readable and well-organized without clinical sterility.

**Draw from:** Article card taxonomy, non-clinical vocabulary as a design principle.

### 6. Eterneva (eterneva.com)
Memorial diamonds brand developed by Hoodzpah. "Bright, personal, yet elevated." Centers the life lived, not the death.

**Draw from:** Warm luminous photography, copy that reframes memorial as celebration, cohesive brand identity system.

### 7. Finch: Self-Care Pet App
Self-care gamification where your progress grows a virtual pet bird. "Cozy daily ritual."

**Draw from:** Daily card UI that feels like an invitation, progress visualization tied to emotional meaning, the "show up for something you care for" mechanic.

### 8. ClearPath Framer Template (clearpath-template.framer.website)
Therapy/coaching template, 8 pre-built pages including a journal section. The closest structural layout reference.

**Draw from:** Hero hierarchy, journal card grid, section navigation patterns.

### 9. Headspace (post-2024 rebrand)
Mental health app that went warm and expressive rather than clinical. Custom typeface, expanded color palette keyed to emotional states.

**Draw from:** Color as emotional signal, onboarding as conversation, consistent illustration style.

### 10. Online-Tribute Memorial Sites (online-tribute.com)
100+ elegant memorial designs. "Forest tones fading into fog," flowing textures.

**Draw from:** Botanical blur backgrounds, typography for memorial contexts, gentle scroll-reveal animations.
