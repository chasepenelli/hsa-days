# Illustration Prompts

Copy-pasteable prompts for generating each illustration. Use with any AI image generator (Midjourney, DALL-E, Flux, Qwen-Image, etc.).

## Shared Style Prefix

Prepend this to every prompt:

> Simple hand-drawn ink sketch illustration, cute and warm, minimal line art style, [COLOR] ink wash accents on white background, editorial book illustration, soft and intimate, no text, no watermarks —

## Shared Negative Prompt

> text, watermark, copyright, blurry, photorealistic, 3D render, anime, cartoon, digital art, neon colors, dark background, letters, words, writing, busy, cluttered
